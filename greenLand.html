GREENLAND / ANTARCTICA ELEVATION


Dataset you are currently viewing: 2003

About this dataset:-

The shades on this map show how high above sea level
are the land surfaces and ice sheets on Greenland
and Antarctica. White shows the highest land and
ice surfaces--up to 4,000 meters above sea
level. Light blue and dark blue show progressively
lower land and ice surfaces. Light blue areas are
about 2,000 meters above sea level. Dark blue shows sea level.
